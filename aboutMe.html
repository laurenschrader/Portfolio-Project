About Me..

I have a career background in sales and retail management. I spent about seven years in retail- five of which were spent working for Nordstrom. I began at Nordstrom as a sales associate, and quickly realized how much I enjoyed the dynamic, creative, and often fast-paced nature of working retail.

At Nordstrom, I moved up into various management positions where I developed my leadership, organization, and problem-solving skills. Retail management was an especially fun space to work in, as every day presented new & interesting challenges to tackle. Needless to say, I quickly developed the skills needed to navigate that sort of fast-paced & dynamic work environment. In short, my time with Nordstrom gave me the opportunity & skills necessary to manage a team, handle logistics, & effectively tackle the always-evolving challenges of a retail space.

Before ever moving into my retail career, I did complete some college education. However at the time, I wasn’t quite clear on which path I wanted to take. It was towards the end of my retail career that I began exploring an interest in computer science. I had watched 2 of my brothers successfully complete Epicodus & shortly after, they moved into their own careers in computer science. Watching their success, ultimately inspired me to join Epicodus & I’m excited to utilize the skills I’ve gathered through my career, as I transition into this new field!
